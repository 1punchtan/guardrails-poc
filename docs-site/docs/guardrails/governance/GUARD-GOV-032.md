## Description

Primary New Zealand legislation establishing the legal framework for the care, protection, and well-being of children and young persons. Defines powers and duties of the chief executive (Oranga Tamariki—Ministry for Children), establishes family group conference procedures, and sets out grounds and processes for care and protection orders.
