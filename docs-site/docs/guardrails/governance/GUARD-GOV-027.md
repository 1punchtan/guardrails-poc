## Description

New Zealand legislation establishing the legal framework for superannuation entitlements, the New Zealand Superannuation Fund, the Guardians of New Zealand Superannuation, and the Retirement Commissioner. Administered by the Ministry of Social Development and The Treasury, it defines age and residential qualifications, payment structures, fund management, and oversight mechanisms for retirement income.
