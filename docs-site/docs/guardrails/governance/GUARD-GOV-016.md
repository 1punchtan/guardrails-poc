## Description

New Zealand government standard (effective 1 July 2025) mandating how public service agencies must establish information sharing agreements with non-government third parties when sharing personal information. Covers purpose, scope, implementation guidance, risk assessment, legal requirements, and template clauses to ensure adequate controls and privacy protection.
