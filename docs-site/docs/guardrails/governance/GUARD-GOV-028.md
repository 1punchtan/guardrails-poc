## Description

New Zealand legislation consolidating human rights protections, anti-discrimination provisions, and complaint resolution mechanisms. Establishes the Human Rights Commission and Human Rights Review Tribunal with authority over employment, goods/services, housing, education, and other discrimination matters across both public and private sectors.
